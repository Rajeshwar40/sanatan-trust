Who We Are

Our Mission & Purpose

A grassroots community trust built on values of service, compassion, and collective action.

📚

Education First

We believe education is the most powerful tool to transform lives. Our trust runs free tutoring centres, scholarships, and skill-development workshops for underprivileged youth.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Through regular medical camps, medicine drives, and linkages with specialists, we ensure that cost is never a barrier to good health for any community member.

🤝

Unity & Trust

We foster a culture of mutual respect, transparency, and collective decision-making. Every rupee collected is accounted for, and every major decision is voted upon by the community.

⚖️

Accountability

Monthly financial reports are shared with all members. Our fund management follows a strict 50-50 model ensuring operational efficiency alongside an always-ready emergency reserve.

How We Manage Funds

Transparency & Accountability

Every rupee is accounted for. Your trust is our greatest asset.

💰

Monthly Contribution

₹500 /member

Every member contributes a fixed monthly amount to sustain and grow our community programs.

Fund Allocation

50% Operational
Use
50% Emergency
Reserve

A strict 50-50 model ensures smooth operations while keeping a safety net for emergencies.

📊

Monthly Reports

Detailed income & expenditure reports shared with all members at the end of every month — nothing hidden, everything documented.

🗳️

Voting-Based Decisions

Major fund disbursements and program decisions are put to a community vote — ensuring democratic and accountable governance at every step.
